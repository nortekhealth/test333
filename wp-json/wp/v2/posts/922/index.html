What is a PSA Oxygen Generator?<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) oxygen generators use clean, compressed air as the raw material and high-quality zeolite molecular sieve as the adsorbent to produce oxygen. The process involves pressure adsorption and decompression desorption in the adsorber, where nitrogen is preferentially adsorbed by the zeolite molecular sieve due to its faster diffusion rate, allowing oxygen to be enriched in the gas phase. The system typically features two adsorption towers that alternate between adsorption and regeneration, controlled by a PLC program controller, to produce oxygen with a purity of approximately 93%\u00b13%.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Features and Technical Specifications<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Features:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Quick start-up: Provides qualified oxygen within 15-30 minutes.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Fully automated operation: Requires no supervision.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Efficient molecular sieve packing: Ensures a longer service life.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Adjustable pressure, purity, and flow rate: Meets diverse customer needs.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Low energy consumption: Ensures economical operation.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Technical Specifications:<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n
      \n
    • Oxygen volume: 1\uff5e300Nm\u00b3\/hr<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Purity: 93%\u00b13%<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Pressure: 0.01~0.6 Mpa (adjustable)<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Atmospheric dew point: -40\u00b0C~-70\u00b0C (adjustable)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      How to Choose a PSA Oxygen Generator<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

      When selecting a PSA oxygen generator, consider the following core parameters:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

        \n
      • Flow rate (Nm\u00b3\/h):<\/strong> Represents the amount of gas consumption required.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Oxygen purity:<\/strong> Indicates the proportion of oxygen in the produced gas.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Pressure:<\/strong> Refers to the outlet pressure of the equipment, typically 0.3-0.5MPa.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Dew point:<\/strong> Represents the water content in the gas; the lower the dew point, the less water present.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

        Maintenance Tips<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

        Proper maintenance is crucial for the longevity and efficiency of the equipment:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

          \n
        • Ensure normal supply of power, water, air, and temperature conditions.<\/li>\n\n\n\n

        • Check the automatic drain valves daily to prevent clogging.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
        • Clean the heat sink of the cold dryer weekly to maintain effectiveness.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
